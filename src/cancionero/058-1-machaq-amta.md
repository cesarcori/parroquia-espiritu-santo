Jilir jilasaw jiwasaru  
Jañchipa t'ant'ap manq'ayistu  
Jesús jilasan sutipxaruw  
Ma wawaki tukupxtan  
  
Jilir jilasaw jiwasaru  
Uvasa wilap umt'ayistu  
Wawanakapax taqiniwa  
Machaq amta phuqhapxtan  
  
Llakinakas apanukutaw  
kusisiña jikxatta  
ma wawar tukt'ata markaxa  
ch'amaniruw tukupxtan  
  
jan mayja mayj uñanaqamti  
diosaw chuymasankaski  
chachasa, warmisa, wawasa  
machaq jaquiruw tuktan
